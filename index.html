FAQ

Questions
we get a lot

Still unsure? We're happy to talk through any of this on a free discovery call.

How long does the whole process take? +
Most projects launch in exactly 14 days. We've built this timeline through hundreds of projects — it's structured, not rushed. Day 1–2: strategy. Day 3–7: design. Day 8–13: build. Day 14: you're live.
What do I need to provide? +
Your branding (logo, colors if you have them), any existing content you want to keep, and answers to our onboarding questionnaire. We handle the rest — design, copy guidance, images, build, and launch.
Do I own the website after? +
Completely. The code, the design assets, the domain — all yours. No lock-in, no monthly platform fees, no strings attached. You can host it wherever you like.
What if I'm not happy with the design? +
We include two structured revision rounds in the design phase. In practice, clients love what we produce. But if something isn't right, we iterate until it is — we don't invoice for revisions.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch? +
Yes. We offer monthly support retainers for content updates, design tweaks, performance monitoring, and growth strategy. Most clients stay with us long-term — our average relationship is 3 years.
