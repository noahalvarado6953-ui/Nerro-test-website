FAQ

Questions
we get a lot

Still unsure? We're happy to talk through this on a free discovery call.

How long does the whole process take?+
Most projects launch in exactly 14 days. Day 1–2: strategy. Day 3–7: design. Day 8–13: build. Day 14: you're live.
What do I need to provide?+
Your branding (logo, colors if you have them), any existing content you want to keep, and answers to our onboarding questionnaire. We handle the rest — design, copy guidance, images, build, and launch.
Do I own the website after?+
Completely. The code, the design assets, the domain — all yours. No lock-in, no monthly platform fees, no strings attached.
What if I'm not happy with the design?+
We include two structured revision rounds in the design phase. If something isn't right, we iterate until it is — we don't invoice for revisions.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?+
Yes. Monthly support retainers for content updates, design tweaks, performance monitoring, and growth strategy. Our average client relationship is 3 years.
